why boozefree here?

It's chilled & light-filled during the day. You could bring your laptop & work over a Reuben sandwhich (they're good, trust me). I get the feeling staff wouldn't mind if you snuck your dog upstairs into the beer garden.

It's down an alley that's filled with graffiti art, in the 🖤 of Footscrazy (Nicholson St mall), above a vinyl shop/cafe, barber & some of the best pizza in the West Melbourne (hi Slice Shop 👋🏼). I am not cool enough to go to this place at night, but based on their Facebook pics & lineup of DJs, seems like it's pretty busy.
