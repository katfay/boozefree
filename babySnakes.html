why here?

It's chilled & light-filled during the day. You could bring your laptop and work over a Reuben sandwhich (they're good, trust me). I get the feeling staff wouldn't mind if you snuck your dog upstairs into the beer garden.

Down an alley that's filled with graffiti art, in the 🖤 of Footscray (Nicholson St mall), above a vinyl shop/cafe and some of the best pizza in the West Melbourne (hi Slice Shop). Seems like at night, based on their Facebook pics and lineup of DJs, it's a pretty busy place too.
